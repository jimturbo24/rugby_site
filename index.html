Who we are

We are a group of individuals with high level spinal cord injuries (SCI) who get together on a weekly basis to play a game, using special wheelchairs, that allows us to continue to be competitive athletes. Half of our members are former members of the military, while the other half are normal non-service citizens. We all share the same common experiences of living a life with an SCI, which just happens to include a love for the game that we play together.

our mission

We endeavor to encourage physical activity and provide community for disabled individuals, promote wheelchair rugby as a sport, provide training in the sport of wheelchair rugby, and solicit financial support to promote the health and welfare of team members through the sport of wheelchair rugby.
